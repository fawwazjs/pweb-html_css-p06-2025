Hayao Miyazaki

Animator · Director · Screenwriter · Producer

About

Hayao Miyazaki (born January 5, 1941) is a Japanese animator, director, producer, screenwriter, author, and co-founder of Studio Ghibli. He is regarded as one of the greatest animation directors in the world, known for his richly imaginative storytelling, environmental themes, and strong character development. His films have inspired generations and earned international acclaim.

Experience

  • Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind (1984) – Director & Screenwriter
  • My Neighbor Totoro (1988) – Director
  • Kiki’s Delivery Service (1989) – Director
  • Princess Mononoke (1997) – Director
  • Spirited Away (2001) – Director (Academy Award Winner for Best Animated Feature)
  • Howl’s Moving Castle (2004) – Director
  • The Wind Rises (2013) – Director
  • The Boy and the Heron (2023) – Director
