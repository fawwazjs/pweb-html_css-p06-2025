Hayao Miyazaki is a renowned Japanese animator, filmmaker, and manga artist. He is the co-founder of Studio Ghibli and has directed numerous acclaimed films such as Spirited Away, My Neighbor Totoro, and Princess Mononoke. Known for his imaginative storytelling and environmental themes, Miyazaki has captivated audiences worldwide with his magical worlds and memorable characters.
