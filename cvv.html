About Me

An internationally acclaimed animation director, producer, screenwriter, animator, author, and manga artist. Co-founder of Studio Ghibli, known for crafting deeply imaginative and visually stunning animated features that explore complex themes of nature, humanity, and personal growth.

Experience

Co-Founder & Director, Studio Ghibli

1985 – Present | Koganei, Tokyo, Japan
  • Co-founded Studio Ghibli, becoming a global leader in animated film production.
  • Directed Spirited Away, Princess Mononoke, My Neighbor Totoro, Castle in the Sky.
  • Pioneered animation techniques and narrative structures influencing generations.

Animator & Director, Topcraft/Nippon Animation

1971 – 1985 | Tokyo, Japan
  • Directed *Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind*, leading to Ghibli’s creation.
  • Contributed to *Lupin the 3rd Part I* and *Future Boy Conan*.
